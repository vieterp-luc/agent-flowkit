## 2. Customer Persona

**Demographics**
- Age: 25–35, urban (HCM, Hanoi, Da Nang)
- Income: 10–25M VND/month, middle-to-upper middle class
- Parenthood stage: 0–5 years old (primary segment)

**Pain Points**
1. **Safety & authenticity** — Fear of fake formula/counterfeit baby products
2. **Quality assurance** — Needs visible proof, reviews, demo of benefit
3. **Convenience** — 1-click add-to-cart, fast delivery

- **YouTube**: Longer reviews, how-to, expert opinion
- **Instagram**: Visual inspiration, lifestyle context
- **Shopee feed/livestream**: Last-mile trust (reviews, Q&A visible)

**Decision Influencers**
- Self (mother) — 60%
- Spouse — 20%
- Mother-in-law / parents — 15%
- Mom-group consensus — 10–15%

## 3. Winning Content Strategy (2025–2026)

**Format Performance**

| Format | Length | Convert Rate | Best For | Example |
|--------|--------|--------------|----------|---------|
| Unboxing/Demo | 15–30s | ★★★★★ | Any product, visual | Open diaper pack, show absorbency |
| Before/After | 20–40s | ★★★★★ | Skincare, solutions | Baby rash → healed skin (day 3/7) |
| Hands-On Test | 25–45s | ★★★★☆ | Toys, gear, tools | Test stroller fold, gate lock, carrier comfort |
| Quick Comparison | 20–30s | ★★★★☆ | Multiple SKUs | Brand A vs B (price, thickness, smell) |
| AI Voiceover (Spec) | 15–25s | ★★☆☆☆ | Low-engagement categories | Vitamin, supplement (static product shots) |

**Optimal Hook (First 3 Seconds)**
- **Problem reveal**: "Bé tôi đổi bỉm 8 lần/ngày, mẹ kiệt sức" (baby needs 8 diapers/day)
- **Visual surprise**: Baby's reaction, product malfunction, instant result
- **Question**: "Có bao giờ bỉm rò không? Tôi test ngay..."
- **Micro-trend alignment**: Parent fail, relatable moment, humor

**Caption & Hashtag Pattern**
```
Hook text (20–30 words): problem + curiosity
[Link in bio] shopee affiliate link
#mẹvàbé #babycareVN #testngay #babyproducts #shopeeaffiliate #mẹtươngtrẻ #review #unboxing
```

**Posting Cadence**
- **Frequency**: 3–5 videos/week minimum (TikTok algorithm favors consistency)
- **Best times**: 19:00–22:00 (evening scroll), 8:00–10:00 (morning routine)
- **Peak days**: Wed–Thu, Sat–Sun (mom downtime)

**Personal Appearance**
- **Recommended**: Show face + voice — authenticity + trust premium
- **AI voiceover fallback**: Use if targeting 100+ videos; lower CTR but scalable
- **Hands-only approach**: Works for product demo if wearing mom/home aesthetic (rings, apron, baby gear visible)

## 6. Compliance & Pitfalls

**Shopee Affiliate Policy**
- Must disclose: "Đây là link Shopee Affiliate" or #shopeeaffiliate in captions
- No false health claims (formula cures illness, milk boosts IQ, skincare cures rash)
- No before/after claims without medical substantiation
- Ban if: Counterfeit promotion, unsafe products, misleading reviews

**Vietnam Advertising Law 2025 (Decree 117)**
- **Mandatory disclosure**: #ads, #sponsored, or "Nội dung quảng cáo" text overlay
- Influencers must verify product authenticity & quality
- No misleading info (false origin, fake user testimonials)
- Government regulates influencer/affiliate roles formally (first time in VN)

**TikTok Shadow Ban Triggers**
- **No disclosure** of affiliate/sponsorship → Shadowban 30–90 days
- **Over-posting links** (>1 per 5 videos) → Reduced FYP visibility
- **Rapid URL clicks** with low engagement → Bot detection
- **Frequent "Shop Now" CTAs** → Algorithm deprioritization
- **Low watch time** on product-heavy videos → Algorithm learns low interest

**FTC / International Compliance**
- If targeting US/Western audience: Use #ad or #sponsored clearly in caption/text overlay
- TikTok's June 2025 update: Promo codes, brand tags = paid endorsements (need disclosure)
- Affiliate links = material connection → "Clear and conspicuous" disclosure required
