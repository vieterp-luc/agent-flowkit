## Brief reference for /fk-create-project

For each day, pass to `/fk-create-project`:

```
Project name:   Petit Atelier — [Title without emoji/duration]
Material:       realistic
Orientation:    HORIZONTAL (long-form, 1920×1080)
Story:          [Description hook from brief above, expanded 3-5 sentences]
Scenes:         1 (long-form loop, single scene)
Entities:
  - location: atelier_setting — [Visual concept setting + background from brief]
  - visual_asset: focal_objects — [Foreground items from brief]
```

Audio gen via `/fk-gen-music` with prompt = `[Genre + BPM] + [Layers from brief]`.
